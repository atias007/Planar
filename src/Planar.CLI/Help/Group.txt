usage: planar-cli group <command> [<options>]

the options for <command> and [<options>] parameters of group module:

 <command>       <options>
------------------------------------------------------
 ls|list 
 get             <id>
 add             <group name>
 update          <id> <property name> <property value>
 remove|delete   <id>
 join            <id> <user id>
 exclude         <id> <user id>
 -----------------------------------------------------
